Dubai South

Dubai South is a 145 sq. km master-planned community near Al Maktoum International Airport and Expo City, designed as a global hub for aviation, logistics, and commerce. It seamlessly integrates residential, commercial, and industrial zones.

Offering apartments, townhouses, and villas, Dubai South provides modern amenities, green spaces, and recreational facilities to support a vibrant lifestyle.

With dedicated business districts and advanced infrastructure, it drives Dubai's economic vision and attracts investors, businesses, and residents seeking long-term growth.
